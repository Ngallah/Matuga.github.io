Overview

Matuga Farmers Group (MFG) was formed in 2015 to enhance local farmers interests, within Matuga Sub-County. The Groups objective is to boost farmers productivity and enhance their living standards by adopting agribussines.

Mission Statement
p>To project, promote and protect local farmers interests through innovative and effective farming techniques to improve productivity and food security.
Vission Statement

To create economic opportunity to members and improve local farmers living standards.


Core Values

Customer focus: We shall treat our members with courtesy, respect and promptness.

Patriotism: Our staff shall exercise loyalty and uphold allegiance to the members at all times.

Team spirit: We shall promote teamwork to enhance service delivery.

Professionalism: Our members of staff shall exercise high level of professional competence and confidentiality in all their work.

Ethics and Integrity: We will embrace transparency and accountability in all operations of the Group.

Equity and fairness: We shall promote justice, impartiality and diversity in all our dealings.
